LIM WEN HAO

Lim Wen Hao is a university student, currently pursuing the foundation of Computing and IT at Asia Pacific University (APU) of Technology and Innovation, Malaysia, while planning to further his studies towards the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI). He has been programming since the age of 13, when he was a student at SMJK Jit Sin, one of Malaysia's top ten secondary schools. By doing massive research on the Internet, going through immense videos on self-studying platforms such as Udemy and YouTube, he is able to master the art of coding from low-level to high-level programming languages. He is best known for his front-end web development skills, using tools such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript with extensions such as Bootstrap, jQuery and Firebase. With a keen interest in the programming field and a sharp vision on the future global shift towards the world of AI and cybersecurity, he aims to look into the power of programming languages including Python and C++. Having good programming skills is not required for every high school student, but it is a good starting point for him to start his own projects.

With the needs to show his talent and presentation skills, he keeps on learning the new video editing skills, aiming to follow up the trend of market. He has been working with his projects with a vast amount of video editor software. Among of the most favourite, Adobe Premiere Pro for video trimming, Adobe After Effects for visual effect (VFX), and Davinci Resolve to make colour adjustment and correction.

A late of getting in touch with new knowledge does not mean one has to stop his movement in life. Despite receiving his personal computer at the age of 14, he tends to develop proficient computer literacy skills, knowing application software such as Photoshop in image processing, Autodesk Fusion and Fritzing in Computer-Aided Design (CAD).
