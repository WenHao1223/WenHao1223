PROJECTS - Innovation & Invention

THE EYE SAVER(2018)

People generally spend an average of 3 hours and 15 minutes on their phones every day, with the top 20% of smartphone users spending upwards of 4½ hours, according to research done by RescueTime, one of several apps for iOS and Android created to monitor phone use. The Eye Saver (TES) aims to reduce the risk of encountering eye diseases as well as correct the bad habits of users when using phones everyday, protecting your precious eyes.

SOS WATCH(2017)

The idea is sparked because of the unexpected report of the Sabah Earthquake in 2015, with a magnitude of 6.9, which has never before occurred in Malaysia. With a reverence for life, the team created SOS Watch, with the goal of assisting rescuers in their search for survivors of disasters ranging from minor to major.
